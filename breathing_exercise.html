Let's Take a Deep Breath

Sometimes, taking a few deep breaths can help us feel calmer. Let's try it together!

1. Breathe in slowly through your nose for 4 seconds.

2. Hold your breath for 4 seconds.

3. Breathe out slowly through your mouth for 4 seconds.

4. Wait for 4 seconds before you breathe in again.

Repeat this a few times. You can close your eyes if it helps you focus.
